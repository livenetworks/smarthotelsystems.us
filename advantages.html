Advantages

With Smart Hotel System your hotel get advantages ahead of the competition:

  • Directly, with decreasing expenses and power bills
  • Indirectly, with increasing the comfort and satisfaction of your guests
  • Smart Hotel System controller at any time knows who is currently in the room.
  • Smart Hotel System system allows functionalities in the room depending on whose card is in the card holder.
  • The rooms are not heated while the cleaning stuff does their work.
  • The guest cannot enter in an uncleaned room. Smart Hotel System system does not allow the receptionist to issue a room with "unclean" status.
  • The room gets status "ready" to issue, only after the visit of a cleaning stuff member, verified with their card.
  • Hot water, heating and cooling in the room, usage of electrical outlets and TV is available only for the guests.
  • Smart Hotel System system prevents unchecked use of the rooms and profit losses.
  • Smart Hotel System controller identifies the cards of every staff member, so it always knows who has been where and who takes responsibility for what.
  • Smart Hotel System controller logs names, time, speed and efficiency of each employee according to their cards.
  • Smart Hotel System system maintains predefined temperature of the room (eg. 12оС), so it can be heated instantly when the guest is checked in.
  • Smart Hotel System system maintains higher temperature (eg. 18оС) if the room is issued and the guest leaves the room.
  • Smart Hotel System system has its highest and lowest temperature limit. Above the highest (eg. 23оС), the guest cannot heat the room and if the temperature falls below the lowest (eg. 5оС), the management gets instantly alarmed to prevent freezing.
  • During an SOS signal or fire alarm, Smart Hotel System system obliges an employee to enter the room and check the problem, preventing the safety of the guest to be compromised.
  • Smart Hotel System controller has sound or light signaling for hazardous situation, activated automatically or at the reception.
